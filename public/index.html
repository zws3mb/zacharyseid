The Elevator Speech

I'm a fourth year undergraduate at the University of Virginia studying Classics, Computer Science, and Statistics. I'm also a student leader in the oldest musical organization on grounds, and an athletic leader in the school's fencing team. I find passion in creativity, be it front web design, web service backend in the cloud, mobile development, etc. I find beauty in patterns and structure, be it Zipf's Law of natural language, sentence structure in Latin poetry, or the various trends and explanations exposed by data science and machine learning. I am driven, independent, and dynamic--technically capable, thorough, and enthusiastic--yet, as said Socrates, "I know nothing."

Why this website?

I wanted to make electronically available (and page-rankable!) a better window into my passions and pursuits, and thought that building a website would be both enlightening and fun. Eventually, I may transition this site to more of a blog across my three favorite domains.
